About Us


BookMania is a vibrant and much-anticipated event that celebrates the joy of reading and learning. It brings together book enthusiasts, publishers, authors, and literary experts in one dynamic space, creating an atmosphere brimming with creativity and inspiration. Attendees can explore a diverse range of books, from timeless classics to the latest bestsellers, catering to all ages and interests. The event often features engaging activities like book signings, author talks, storytelling sessions, and workshops, making it an enriching experience for visitors. Beyond the world of books, the fair fosters community connections and promotes the cultural significance of literature, leaving attendees with cherished memories and a renewed love for the written word.
